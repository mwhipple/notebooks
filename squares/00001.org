#+title: Square One

This site will contain a series of "square" posts, where each such
square will have bounds of 80 columns and 80 rows. This is somewhat of
an arbitrary decision, but has been adopted since I otherwise have a
tendency to get too verbose which leads to posts taking too long to
write which in turn results in my regularly halting their creation.
80 columns is a common (albeit old) line length convention for coding
going back to [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Punched_card][punched cards]]. Beyond offering some symmetry, 80 lines
seems like a reasonable length to both support regaular contribution,
enstill some marginal editing discipline, and hopefully also provide a
decent length for anyone else who may read the content.

The content of this site will somewhat be a blog, where each entry is
also subject to subsequent revision. Each square will be posted as a
number with the expectation that at some point additional metadata and
discoverability will be layered on top of that.  The technology used
for the site itself will be incrementally developed (starting with ")
/very/ basic tools). The content will be authored by me - at some
point it is plausible that some AI will be introduced as an editor and
provide feedback, but is expected to be left out of creating or
modifying content directly. Initially I will ostensibly perform such
revision myself...though in practice I tend to do so intermittently
after the content is published so there is likely to be a fair amount
of escaped sloppiness (on top of the baseline sloppiness stemming from
my lack of writing experience).
